    Announcements and Discussion E-mail Lists

    Announcements
    The Open Accessibility Alliance Tools announcements list is for people who would like learn about the latest releases of FAE 2.0. It is a one way low volume e-mail list announcing major changes to the tools or training opportunities to learn more about accessibility. Registered users of FAE are automatically added to this list, but users can remove themselves from the list by updating their User Profile.
    Discussion
    The Open Accessibility Alliance Tools discussion list is for people who would like to have more direct participation in setting the direction for development of FAE 2.0 and discuss issues with the development team. The group also holds a monthly teleconference to discuss user and development issues.
