    Summary of Options

    Option Description
    Individual
    • Single account
    • Mulitple subscription levels are available based on the maximum number of archived reports, spidering depth and number of pages per report.
    • If you have a need that does not fit the current subscription levels, contact the administrator with your needs.
    Institutional
    • Multiple accounts based on InCommon authentication. InCommon is used by most Higher Education Institutions to manage authentication to campus supported IT services.
    • Subscription costs based on the size of the institution.
    • There is one default subscription level for all sizes of institutions, but you can contact the adminstrator to request a custom level for your institution.
